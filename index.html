From the lands of Miramar, Buenos Aires, Argentina, emerges Luca Napoli, a talented musician whose passion for music has been growing since he delved into the world of guitar and singing at a very young age.

While he began his musical career in the metal genre, his love for techno quickly took over. His determination and dedication have taken him all over Argentina and Chile, bringing his music to every corner of the continent. His talent did not go unnoticed, and he began signing with some of the top 10 techno record labels, releasing over 35 tracks, remixes, and collaborations with artists from around the world on all streaming platforms.

Luca's skills as a DJ have allowed him to share the stage with some of the most recognized artists in the industry, including Matt Sasari, MT93, and Ramon Tapia, among others. His contagious energy on stage and ability to create a unique atmosphere in each performance has made him acclaimed by techno fans worldwide.

Despite his success, Luca remains humble and continues to expand his musical knowledge by absorbing new trends to bring to the dance floor. His passion for music has taken him to places he never imagined, and his story continues to be written day by day as he takes his music to the next level.
